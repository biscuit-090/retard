$20,000+ Buys/Sells The $20,000 threshold is an intentionally fixed number. Trades smaller than this are not typically indicative of suspicious behavior or insider activity. Trades at or above this level are rare enough to stand out, and statistically more likely to involve highly confident actors - inside traders.

This filter keeps the feed focused on meaningful suspicious activity, not spam.
